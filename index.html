FAQs

Everything
you need
to know

The nearest airports are Málaga (1 hour drive) and Seville (1.5 hours). Both have excellent connections from the UK and Europe. There's also a scenic train from Málaga or from Seville if you'd prefer to arrive by rail. We'll be sharing more detailed travel notes closer to the date.
Ronda itself has wonderful places to stay. We recommend the Parador de Ronda for something special (book early), or Hotel Catalonia Ronda as a lovely mid-range option. There are also beautiful rural fincas in the surrounding countryside if you'd like to make a proper retreat of it.
Summer formal — let's celebrate colour. The brighter the better!
Yes — if enough people are staying walking distance from Ronda centre, we hope to arrange a shuttle service to/from the venue (10 mins drive) from outside the Parador hotel. This will shuttle from 3–3.30 to ensure all guests are on site and seated by 3.45. It will then run on demand back to Ronda all night. If you are staying outside of Ronda please see taxi information.
Early October in Ronda is warm during the day (22–26°C), clear skies, and cool evenings. Sunscreen during the afternoon ceremony is a good idea.
We love your children dearly, but we've chosen to make our day an adults-only celebration (18+) so that everyone — parents most of all — can truly let their hair down. We hope you understand and can arrange childcare. If this presents any difficulty please get in touch and we'll do our best to help.
Honestly, making the trip to Spain with us is the most generous gift we could ask for. If you'd like to contribute something, we have a small honeymoon fund — details will be shared with your paper invitation. No obligation whatsoever.
We would really love this to be a phone-free wedding as much as possible, however appreciate that guests would like to take their own photos — we are happy for you to do this but please ensure phones are away when not taking photos. We have 2 photographers to capture the occasion to ensure you are able to enjoy it in real life and not through your screen!
