Everything

The nearest airports are Málaga (1hr20 drive) and Seville (1hr45 drive). Both have connections from the UK and Europe. There's a bus and scenic train from Málaga or if you'd prefer to arrive by rail. If you're hiring a car, be aware that parking in Ronda is very limited, check if your accommodation offers it. We're happy to help coordinate group taxis or a bus, grouping guests by flight arrivals to make things as easy as possible.
Ronda has wonderful places to stay at every price point. Book early — October fills up fast. Here are some options we'd recommend:
  • Parador de Ronda
  • Casa Palacio VillaZambra
  • Soho Boutique Palacio San Gabriel
  • Hotel Catalonia Ronda — well-located mid-range hotel in the centre
  • Apartments via Booking.com
  • Airbnb Ronda
Summer formal, with jackets for the men. Most of the event will be on grass so choose your footware wisely.
Yes, we plan to arrange a shuttle service to/from the venue (10 mins drive) departing from the town center. Shuttles will run from roughly 2.30 - 3:15pm to ensure all guests are seated by 3.45pm, then on demand back to Ronda throughout the evening. If you're staying outside Ronda, we recommend a local taxi.
Early October in Ronda is warm during the day (20–26°C), clear skies, and cool evenings. Please bring sun protection for during the day as there is limited shade, and ensure something warm for sitting out later in the evening.
